    3 Steps to Stagelighter

    1. Upload your material.
    2. Create a 'Set'. A 'Set' is a combination of your headshot, resume, a short introduction video and a video of you performing a song or monologue.
    3. Choose your coach.

    That's it! The professionals that you select to work with will carefully look over your material and personally respond to you, usually within 2 weeks. Your coaching will consist of a video introduction from your coach followed by responses to questions designed to help you improve every aspect of your performance. If you do not receive your coaching within 30 days, you can request a 100% refund.

    Pricing

    Pricing on Stagelighter is simple. Membership is free and coachings start at just $59 each. There has never been a more affordable way to connect with the most knowledgeable and experienced professionals in the industry.

    So have fun, learn lots and break a leg without breaking the bank.

    Stagelighter Coaching Questions

    Every time you have an online coaching, the professional that you select to work with will carefully look over your material and answer the following questions:

    • Thoughts on the Performer's headshot
    • Thoughts on the Performer's resume?
    • Was the song/monologue selection a good choice for the Performer?
    • What age span can the Performe play?
    • Performance Rating
    • Comments on performance
    • Comments on appropriateness of hair, makeup and attire for an audition
    • What should the Performer work on for next time?
    • One question of the Performer's choice
